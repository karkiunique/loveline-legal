Privacy Policy

Loveline · Last updated: April 20, 2026

Loveline ("we", "our", "the app") helps couples build a shared timeline of their memories. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, email us at uniquekarki101@gmail.com.

1. Information we collect

  • Account info: email address and password (hashed) when you sign up, and your display name and avatar color.
  • Couple info: the couple name and anniversary date, if you choose to add them.
  • Moment content you create: titles, dates, descriptions, emoji, mood tags, card colors, photos, voice memos, approximate or precise location (only when you attach one), and metadata about any Spotify track you link to a moment.
  • Private notes: optional notes you mark as private are stored encrypted at rest and visible only to you.
  • Device data: basic technical information such as app version, operating system, and crash logs, used to diagnose issues.

We do not collect advertising identifiers, we do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not sell personal information.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate the app: sign you in, sync your timeline with your partner, display your moments, and deliver notifications you opt into.
  • To search Spotify when you attach a song to a moment. Your query is sent to Spotify via our server; Spotify's privacy policy applies to how Spotify handles those requests.
  • To show maps and place suggestions when you attach a location, using Google Maps services.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve reliability.
  • To respond to you when you contact support.

3. Who can see your content

Moments, photos, voice memos, and the timeline are shared with the partner you invite to your couple. Private notes are visible only to you. We do not make your content public. Loveline staff do not read your content except as strictly necessary to investigate a reported bug, abuse report, or legal request.

4. Where your data is stored

Data is stored with our hosting provider (Supabase), which operates on infrastructure in the United States and Europe. Photos and voice memos are stored in private buckets and served via signed URLs. Backups are retained for a limited period for disaster recovery.

5. Your choices and rights

  • Access and export: you can view all of your content in the app at any time.
  • Delete content: you can delete individual moments, photos, and voice memos from inside the app.
  • Delete your account: go to Settings and choose "Delete account," or email uniquekarki101@gmail.com. We remove your profile and content within 30 days, except where we are required to retain limited records for legal or security reasons.
  • Location and microphone: you can revoke permissions at any time from your device's system settings; features that need them will stop working.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA, including the right to correct, port, or object to processing of your data. Contact us at uniquekarki101@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

6. Children

Loveline is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest for backups and private notes, hashed passwords, and row-level security rules on our database. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-party services

Loveline uses the following third-party services to provide features. Each has its own privacy policy.

  • Supabase — authentication, database, file storage
  • Spotify — music search and track metadata
  • Google Maps Platform — map display and location search
  • Expo — app delivery and push notifications

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you inside the app. Continued use of Loveline after an update means you accept the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: uniquekarki101@gmail.com.
